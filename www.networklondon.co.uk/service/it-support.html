Frequently asked questions

What is managed IT support?

Managed IT support is a comprehensive service where an external provider, like ours, takes care of all your IT needs. This includes monitoring and maintaining your IT systems, resolving technical issues, and providing strategic guidance to ensure your technology aligns with your business goals. Think of it as having your own IT department, but without the overheads. With managed IT support, you can focus on running your business, knowing that your technology is in expert hands.

Who needs IT support?

IT support is essential for any business that relies on technology – which is likely every business operating today. Whether you're a small start-up, a growing SME, or a large corporation, efficient and reliable IT support is crucial. It's not just about fixing problems when they arise; it's also about preventing issues, enhancing system performance, and aligning technology with business strategies. In short, if your business uses technology in any capacity, you'll benefit from professional IT support to keep operations running smoothly and to leverage technology for growth and success.

Why use managed IT services?

Using managed IT services offers several key benefits for your business:

  • Expertise and Experience: Managed IT services provide access to a team of experts with a wide range of skills and knowledge. This means you get comprehensive IT support without the cost of hiring a full in-house team.
  • Cost-Effectiveness: Managed IT services can be more cost-effective than maintaining an in-house IT team. You save on the costs associated with hiring, training, and equipping IT staff, and you can opt for a service plan that fits your budget.
  • Proactive Support: Rather than just fixing problems as they occur, managed IT services focus on preventing issues before they impact your business. This proactive approach keeps your systems running smoothly and reduces downtime.
  • Scalability: As your business grows, so do your IT needs. Managed IT services are scalable, meaning we can adjust the level of support as required, ensuring that you always have the right resources in place.
  • Focus on Core Business: With us handling your IT needs, you can focus on what you do best – running your business. This allows you to dedicate more time and resources to areas that drive growth and revenue.

In summary, managed IT services provide expertise, save costs, offer proactive support, are scalable, and allow you to focus on your core business, all of which are crucial for the efficient and effective running of your company.

What does IT support do exactly?

IT support teams, like ours, perform a variety of crucial tasks to ensure your IT systems are efficient, secure, and aligned with your business objectives. Here are a few examples of what we do:

  • Monitoring and Maintenance: We continuously monitor your IT infrastructure to ensure everything continuous to run smoothly. This includes performing ongoing maintenance to prevent potential issues.
  • Troubleshooting and Problem-Solving: When IT issues arise, we're on hand to troubleshoot and resolve them quickly, minimising any disruption to your business.
  • Strategic IT Planning: We provide strategic guidance to ensure your IT setup supports and enhances your business goals, including planning for growth and scalability.
  • Cybersecurity Management: Protecting your data and systems from cyber threats is paramount. Our advanced cyber security services include a number of advanced features for every scenario.
  • Software and Hardware Management: We manage software updates, hardware installations, and integrations, ensuring you have the right tools for your business needs.
  • User Support and Training: Our team is available to assist your staff with any IT-related queries and provide training to ensure they can make the most of your technology.
  • Data Backup and Recovery: We implement data backup solutions and have plans in place for data recovery, so you can have peace of mind knowing your critical business data is safe.

And much more... In essence, our IT support team acts as your complete IT department, handling everything from day-to-day maintenance to strategic planning, always ensuring your technology is a step ahead and supporting your business's success.
