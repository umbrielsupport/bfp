CCTV and Security
Camera Systems

Safeguarding your property

Safeguarding your property

CONTACT US

Having cameras at your property can give you extra peace of mind when you are on or off site, check out how our CCTV service can help you.

At Basingstoke Fire Protection, our CCTV services provide the utmost security to safeguarding your property. All of our security camera systems are tailored specifically for your premises, ensuring universal safety.

Our wireless CCTV systems give you the ability to monitor people and control the movements of the cameras in public, and private buildings. This can be achieved securely from various locations.

We can provide:

  • CCTV for various premises
  • Camera repairs
  • Equipment repairs
  • Wireless camera systems
  • Ongoing maintenance

Businesses are expressing the need for both safety and security in a society where theft, vandalism and violence are unfortunately, a threat. CCTV can help deter thieves and CCTV footage can be used as evidence should it need be.

Based in Hampshire, but serving Berkshire, Surrey and London, Basingstoke Fire Protection has experience providing CCTV and security solutions to large and small companies, institutions and many other properties.

Our engineers have a wealth of technical knowledge and an excellent level of customer service. Contact us today to discuss any security system requirements you have.

Fire Risk Assessment

A Fire Risk Assessment will help to determine what the chances are of a fire occurring in your workplace. In accordance with the Regulatory Reform (Fire Safety) Order, 2005, it is mandatory to carry out a fire risk assessment which must be a written document if more than five people are employed.
